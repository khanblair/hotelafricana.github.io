NILE HALL VENUE

We at Hotel Africana, know and understand the importance of your perfect wedding day. And we understand, that in order to make your special day unique, the secret lies not only in the big things but also down to the smallest detail. The Hotel Africana is the most prestigious wedding venue in Uganda. Offering extensive grounds, a private garden arena, a stand-alone social events center capable of hosting 5000 guests, an elegant ballroom, a roof-top reception terrace and a poolside reception area with dedicated events pavilion, this unique hotel is as capable of hosting an intimate event for just family and friends, as it is a full-scale ceremony in authentic style, with over 1000 guests.
