Dining

NGEYE RESTAURANT

There is a wide choice of tasty dishes ranging from oriental, western and traditional African delicacies under attractive ambiance. With its subtle interior and never ending choice of dishes, there is something for everyone to cherish. Whether it is family get together, party with friends or candle light dinner with someone special, Ngeye Restaurant suits all the occasion.

Opening Hours : 06:00am to 10:00am, 01:00pm to 03:00pm, 07:00pm to 10:00pm

ELGON RESTAURANT

Located inside the lobby, our coffee shop serves hot & delicious breakfast early in the morning for our customers. Enhanced by the legendary and famous Uganda Local coffee Beans, the Coffee Shop will take you through a journey of magical experience with different, carefully natured tastes of the finest coffee in Kampala.

Opening Hours : 06:00am to 10:00am, 01:00pm to 03:00pm, 07:00pm to 10:00pm.

PALM GARDEN RESTAURANT

You want a taste of freshness that will leave you wanting more and more, visit the ala carte restaurant. The amazing chicken wings and African tea is something you will never forget at the hotel. so much more at this restaurant that will make your stay worth a while.

Opening Hours : 10:30am to 10:00pm.

BAKERY

Hotel Africana understand the importance of fresh baked products. Therefore it bakes all the baked consumable items in its own inhouse bakery. This helps us to maintain good quality along with proper hygiene. Our experienced staff prepares variety of cakes & pastries for our guests to enjoy.

Opening Hours : 12:00am to 10:00am

SOCIAL CAFE

After a tiring day, if you are looking to quench your thirst then our bartenders can serve the drink of your choice. Enjoy it while watching the live musical event in Hotel Africana. Our bar has the stock of all the popular brands from around the world to take care of our guests.

Opening Hours : 06:00am to 11:00pm.
